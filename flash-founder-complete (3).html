The CAMP Framework

How VCs really evaluate startups

Four pillars that drive funding decisions. Master these, and you'll know exactly what investors look for.

Learn more about CAMP →
💰

Capital

Your financial health, burn rate, funding history, and capital efficiency. VCs need to see you can manage money before they give you theirs.

⚡

Advantage

What makes you different? Your moats, IP, unique insights, and why competitors can't just copy you tomorrow.

📈

Market

TAM, growth rates, competition, and timing. VCs need to see a big enough opportunity that's ready for your solution.

👥

People

Your team's experience, founder-market fit, and ability to execute. The #1 reason VCs pass is team concerns.
