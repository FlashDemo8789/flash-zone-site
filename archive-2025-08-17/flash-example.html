Top 3 Reasons VCs Will Pass

The critical issues you need to fix before fundraising

1

Unclear Competitive Advantage

Impacts: Advantage

Your pitch positions CloudSync as "Dropbox for enterprises" but doesn't explain why enterprises would choose you over established players. VCs see no defensible moat or unique technology.

Why VCs Care:
Without clear differentiation, you'll face an uphill battle against better-funded competitors. VCs need to see how you'll win and defend market share.
How to Fix:
Focus on your unique compliance features for healthcare. Build case studies showing 10x faster HIPAA compliance vs competitors. Get 2-3 healthcare IT leaders as advisors to validate approach.
2

Weak Go-to-Market Strategy

Impacts: Market Capital

"We'll use content marketing and outbound sales" isn't a strategy. You have no specific channels identified, no CAC projections, and no proof of early traction.

Why VCs Care:
Customer acquisition is the #1 killer of B2B startups. Without a proven path to customers, VCs assume you'll burn through cash experimenting.
How to Fix:
Run 3 channel experiments (e.g., healthcare conferences, partner integrations, direct outreach to IT directors). Document conversion rates and CAC. Build a bottoms-up revenue model.
3

Missing Commercial Experience

Impacts: People

Your team has 3 engineers but no one who's sold B2B software or built partnerships. This creates execution risk around your go-to-market.

Why VCs Care:
Great products fail without distribution. VCs need confidence you can actually reach and sell to enterprise customers.
How to Fix:
Add a commercial co-founder or senior sales hire with enterprise SaaS experience. Alternative: Bring on 2-3 advisors who've scaled B2B sales teams from 0 to $10M ARR.
