The CAMP Framework

The science behind smart fundraising

Our CAMP framework reveals what VCs actually evaluate when making investment decisions

💰

Capital

Your financial health, burn rate, funding history, and capital efficiency. VCs need to see you can manage money before they give you theirs.

⚡

Advantage

What makes you different? Your moats, IP, unique insights, and why competitors can't just copy you tomorrow.

Market

TAM, growth rates, competition, and timing. VCs need to see a big enough opportunity that's ready for your solution.

Your team's experience, founder-market fit, and ability to execute. The #1 reason VCs pass is team concerns.

FAQ

Common questions

How does FLASH work?
FLASH uses the CAMP framework to evaluate your startup across four critical dimensions: Capital, Advantage, Market, and People. We analyze 45 key metrics that investors care about most, then provide you with a fundability score and specific recommendations for improvement.
Who built FLASH and why?
FLASH was built by founders who've experienced the fundraising struggle firsthand. After going through countless rejections and finally raising capital, we realized founders need honest feedback before they pitch. We created FLASH to democratize access to the insights that typically only come from expensive advisors or painful rejections.
Do I need a pitch deck to use FLASH?
No! That's the beauty of FLASH. Our guided questionnaire helps you share the right information about your startup without needing a polished deck. In fact, this can be a great way to understand what should go in your pitch deck when you do create one.
What is the FLASH Fund?
The FLASH Fund is our investment vehicle that provides $100K-$500K to high-scoring startups. If you achieve a fundability score of 75 or higher, you'll unlock the ability to apply for investment. We already understand your business from the analysis, so our diligence process is streamlined, with decisions in just 2 weeks.
Is my data secure and confidential?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow security best practices to protect your data. Your information is only visible to you and is never shared, sold, or used for any purpose other than providing your analysis. We're committed to maintaining the highest security standards to protect sensitive startup information.
What stage startups is this for?
FLASH works best for pre-seed to Series A startups. If you're raising your first $500K to $10M, our analysis will be most relevant. Our AI adapts its evaluation based on your stage, applying stage-appropriate metrics and benchmarks.
How much will it cost?
Early access members will receive exclusive pricing and benefits. We'll share detailed pricing when we launch, but we're committed to making this accessible for bootstrapped founders while delivering exceptional value. Think of it as a fraction of what you'd spend on one advisor meeting.
Can I re-run analysis after improvements?
Yes! Most founders run multiple analyses as they improve their pitch. Track your progress over time and see your score increase. Pro plans include unlimited analyses so you can iterate quickly.
